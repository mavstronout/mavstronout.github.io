Roclineage: Social Linked Data for Passive Income

The Roclineage collaboration is geared towards innovative business solutions to reclaim data rights to earn tokens for our contributions online.
As a decentralized and eclectic group of technical, media and economic minds we are involved in our communities to debate, understand and create solutions.
Our milestones in-progress include use of Hyperledger DLT technology as a proof-of-concept for voting, community and online trust; tokenomic systems to reward trust, provide welfare, connect others and reward community contributions for their data.
Our method isn't geared to individuals exclusively, but rather influential and benefit the network effect using data driven methodology.

Open Menu Ordering App

This application does some impressive things to demonstrate the power of an ORM. The first version of the restaurant uses a custom hand-built ORM to route the web app to the MySQL database.
First create whatever you would like to order and submit. Then click on the item to update the database and move it to the devoured column using Handlebars.
Revamped and brought back with customer associations, the sequel to "Eat the Burger" requires sequelize to connect to the backend. Deployed to Heroku, projects running on sleeping dynos may take time to load.
